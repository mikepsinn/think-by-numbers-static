The recent uptick in drug deaths is not just due to opioids. Deaths from opioids have increased by 753% since 1999, but deaths from cocaine and methamphetamines have increased by approximately 456% during this same time.

The increase in self-medication using a variety of mind altering drugs suggests that there has been an overall increase in pain and unhappiness, which these drugs mask.
